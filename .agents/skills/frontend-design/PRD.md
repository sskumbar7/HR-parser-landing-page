Product Requirements Document: Intelligent CV Matcher
1. Product Overview
The Intelligent CV Matcher is a candidate parsing and comparison engine designed to eliminate manual resume screening. By allowing HR professionals to score and compare candidate profiles against specific Job Descriptions (JDs), the tool drastically reduces cognitive load and time-to-hire. It provides a centralized, actionable database to help recruiters make rapid, data-backed decisions on who to move into the interview pipeline.

2. Target Audience & User Personas
Primary Users: HR Managers, In-house Recruiters, and Talent Acquisition Leads at mid-to-large-sized organizations.

Core Pain Points:

High cognitive overload from reviewing volumes of generic, unqualified applications.

Difficulty manually mapping a candidate's stated experience to the specific requirements of the JD.

Inefficient workflows, such as toggling between multiple tabs or printing resumes to compare top candidates.

Primary Goal: To quickly identify, filter, and act on the top 5% of applicants for a specific role.

3. Core Features (MVP)
JD Baseline Engine: Users can upload or paste a specific Job Description to define the strict skill and experience parameters for an open role.

Intelligent CV Parsing & Scoring: The system ingests bulk candidate CVs, extracts key data points, and assigns a Match Score (High, Medium, Low) based entirely on alignment with the uploaded JD.

Centralized Candidate Repository: A live, interactive database of all parsed candidates, serving as the single source of truth for the hiring mandate.

Dynamic Filtering & Search: Users can filter the database by Match Score, specific technical skills, years of experience, or notice period to instantly narrow the talent pool.

Multi-Candidate Comparison View: A dedicated UI allowing users to select 2 to 3 candidates and compare their extracted skills, experience gaps, and match scores side-by-side.

Bulk Export & Reporting: The capability to export filtered candidate lists (including match scores and contact details) into standard formats (CSV, Excel) for internal stakeholder review.

4. Future Roadmap (Post-MVP)
Phase 2: ATS Integration: Direct hooks into platforms like Workday or Greenhouse to automate the flow of CVs into the parsing engine.

Phase 3: Automated Engagement: AI-generated outreach templates tailored to each candidate's "Match Score" profile.

Phase 4: Collaborative Review: Features enabling multiple team members to leave comments, ratings, and upvote/downvote candidates directly within the live database.

Phase 5: Bias Mitigation: "Blind Hiring" toggles to automatically obscure demographic data (names, gender, age) from the database and comparison views, ensuring equitable screening.
